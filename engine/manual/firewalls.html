Using POPFile with firewalls

POPFile needs to be given full permissions on your local network and local machine, and permission to access the rest of the Internet. Remember that, since POPFile is programmed in the Perl programming language, you'll need to give permissions to perl.exe, wperl.exe, or your Perl parser on other operating systems.

In ZoneAlarm, you can simply answer 'Yes' to each question and check the 'Always allow...' box.

Important note: this gives all Perl programs permission to act as servers!

There are currently no known issues with POPFile and firewalls, provided it's allowed to connect on the port it requires.
